Vikings

For more then a thousand years the Vikings has fascinated us with their heroic deeds and mythical travels. Their heritage is constantly precent and they still influence our modern world all around the globe. Welcome to embark upon a journey leading you through history to the end of the Iron Age. We are an reenactment group striving to recreate the everyday lives of the remarkable men and women who lived more than a thousand years ago. Whether it is through fighting or craftsmanship we try to relive the lives of the people we know as the Vikings.
