Vikings

Traders, craftspeople, worriers, plunderers, explores, adventurers and pioneers. These are just a few words that describe the fascinating group of people we today call Vikings. On rune stones we recall their lives, battles and travels. We can read about how they influenced the places they came to in written sources from all across the European continent and the Middle east. Even when the Vikings are described as ruthless plunderers ancient writers cannot help to also show their fasciation and respect. We are an reenactment group striving to recreate this fascinating chapter in history and relive the everyday lives of the men and women who walked these lands more than a thousand years ago.

Historical

The Viking Age is one of the most mythical and intriguing periods in history. It is also one of the most debated and stereotypical ones. As reeanactors we conduct a form of experimental archaeology were we, with the help from both archaeological finds and written sources, recreates the Viking Age society on a full scale. Historical authenticity is very important and all our equipment, tools, and clothing should be as correct as possible, always hand made and preferably with an actual find as precursor. To the most of us this is more then just a hobby, it’s a lifestyle and we spend hunders of hours of training fighting techniques, studying and crafting our equipment. In opposition to role play or larps, you don’t have a role to play or scripts to follow, you are just yourself living as you would during the Viking age, without the stress of the modern world.

Fighters

Sword · Axe · Spear

There was a strong warrior culture in the Viking society and weaponry skills and strength were highly respected abilities. Every week, the fighters in Skåne lag draw their swords, axes, spears and shields and fight together. It's a full body contact sport were we fight with real steel weapons, so it's no place for the week one. We train for years and we recreate fighting techniques and gear once used by the Vikings. Fighting requires technique, strength and speed as well as trust in your team and respect for the opponent. Together we prepare for the battlefields where we line up to fight our enemies. In that moment, we stand united under our banner and are part of history.

Craftsmanship

Metal · Jewlery · Cloathing

For over a thousand years the artifacts of the Vikings have been kept below ground. Today, with the help of archaeology and history these objects are rediscovered and studied. Many of the members in Skåne lag have devoted countless hours to learn the crafts from the Viking era. Blacksmiths create the most beautiful jewelry from silver, gold and bronze and mighty weapons from iron. Wool and linen is spun and colored using only authentic methods. The colorful yarn is used in tablet weaving, needle binding and for creating clothes. Just as natural as it once was to our ancestors to craft what they needed, just as natural craftsmanship comes to the reenactment lifestyle and you’ll learn techniques required and used for thousands of years.
